Where was netball invented?
Netball is a relatively old sport which has grown from humble roots to become a major sport throughout the world. The history of netball dates back to the late 1890s, when it was developed in England, following the introduction of basketball a few years earlier. The sport quickly grabbed hold, with women throughout the British empire participating as a way to socialise and stay healthy.
